The
Manifesto
of the
Outlier

I.

The Intent of the Object

We do not build tools; we build extensions of the human spirit. Every line of code and every pixel is a choice. We believe that a product should not just function, but possess a "bicycle-like" efficiency for the mind.

II.

The Cultivation of Taste

We refuse the mediocre. We commit to exposing ourselves to the best things humans have done—across art, science, and history—and bringing that excellence into our craft. Taste is the only non-fungible resource.

III.

The Courage to be a Pirate

It is better to be a pirate than to join the navy. We value the "garage" spirit of agility and rebellion over the stagnation of the system. We understand that "good artists copy, but great artists steal."

IV.

The Science of Poking Life

Everything around us was made up by people no smarter than we are. We do not just inhabit the world; we poke it. We push against the boundaries of the "possible" until they give way to our vision.

V.

The Discipline of the Visionary

We embrace mistakes as the price of ambition and the fuel for enrichment. We harbor no regrets for things left undone, for we act with relentless intent.

Stay hungry.
Stay foolish.
